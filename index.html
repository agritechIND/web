What We Do?

For Input Suppliers

We work directly with manufacturers and suppliers to make available high quality inputs at best price at the Farm Gate.

We know what farmer needs and when. We ensure that your product reaches the farmer in most efficient and timely manner.

For OEM Suppliers

We provide everything growers need on-farm hardware, software, digital agronomy, AI-driven analytics and unbeatable support.

We assist Physical & Digital Tech Companies better understand and accordingly cater the end user - The Farmer.

For Food & Retail

We strictly Monitor all stages of production from sowing till harsveting, conforming to National & International statutary norms.

We help Food Processing & Retail Partners ensure long term assured supply of high quality Agriculture Produce.

For Insurance & Banks

We are primarily a Data Company maintaining and analysing high volumes of General, Demographic and Transactional Data.

We enable Insurance and Financial firms to make personalized crop insurance plans, financial advisory & solutions.

Why Us?

Farmers First

We put Farmers First in everything we do. We are on a mission to make farming profitable by applying Mordern Technology.

Honesty, Fairness and Trust

We partner with farmers to improve productivity, profitability, environmental sustainability and transparency in Agriculture.

Data Privacy Commitment

We respect Farmer privacy and maintain strict policies on how it can be used. We never share Data for third-party profit.

IoT Data Platform

Our IoT enabled platform supports on-farm sensors and weather data to create smart farming recommendations to improve yields.

Crop Lifecycle Monitoring

We strictly Monitor all stages of production from sowing till harsveting. Our advisory helps to predict output and raise profitability.

Best Support Team

Our product and agronomic specialists are on hand 12 hours a day to answer questions via chat, email or phone.
